Review of the movie Pride & Prejudice 2005

Sofia Williams, February 3rd, 2024

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Joe Write's adaption of Pride & Prejudice completely exceeded my expectations, not only my expectations for romance movies but my expectations for love. With a powerful love story and fantastic cast, I easily fell in love with the film. I loved the way the actors potrayed the characters, and how they captured the characters essence. The chemistry between Knightley and Macfayden blew me away, it made me fall in love with the time period genre.

I hope one day to find my own Mr. Darcy. As a massive hopeless romantic, I can't help but to find comfort in these films. With Mr. Darcy finding annoyance in Elizabeth in the beginning to falling completely in love with her, and as he belittles her family in his marriage proposal their enemies-to-loversesc captured my heart. Their love being very gentle and warm with the films color's help set the mood, I found the movie to be extremely beautiful (and Mr.Darcy to be extremely handsome). Finally, I give this movie a 4.75/5 as it's my third favorite movie of all time.
